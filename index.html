What is a Live Puff?

The Art of Fresh Baking

100% Live Baked
Freshness

Baked Fresh, Served Piping Hot

Unlike traditional bakeries where puffs sit on shelves for hours, we prepare and bake every puff live as soon as you order.

01

Gourmet Fillings & Pastry

We layer our light, delicate puff pastry sheets with freshly prepared custom fillings (Paneer, Garlic, Schezwan, and rich home-style sauces).

02

Generous Cheese & Spices

We pack our puffs with cheese or top them with cheese bursts and garnishes according to your choice.

03

Live Oven Baking

The puff is baked in our ovens right before your eyes. You get to hear the sizzle and smell the golden pastry rise!
